## About Me

Hi! I'm **Biao Yi**, a Ph.D. student from AI4GC Lab at Zhejiang University, advised by Shengyu Zhang.

My research focuses on **GUI agents** under **device-cloud collaboration**: enabling lightweight on-device models to handle routine UI interactions while invoking stronger cloud models only when necessary, so that agents remain responsive and cost-efficient in real-world deployment. To make this practical, I also work on **speculative decoding** and other inference-acceleration techniques that close the latency gap between device and cloud.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations on GUI agents, device-cloud systems, or efficient LLM inference.

## Research Directions

- **GUI Agent** — Building agents that perceive, ground, and act on real graphical interfaces across desktop and mobile platforms.
- **Device–Cloud Cooperation** — Designing scheduling and offloading strategies that let on-device agents decide when to query stronger cloud models under latency and cost budgets.
- **Speculative Decoding** — Accelerating LLM and agent inference with draft-and-verify schemes tailored to interactive, latency-sensitive workloads.
