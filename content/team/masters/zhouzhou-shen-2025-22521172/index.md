## About Me

Hi! I'm **Zhouzhou Shen**, a master's student at AI4GC Lab, Zhejiang University, advised by Shengyu Zhang.

My research focuses on computer-using agents and efficient multimodal AI systems. I am especially interested in building web agents that can anticipate, verify, and correct their actions with world models, making interactive automation more reliable in real web environments.

I also work on efficient multimodal long-context inference, including modality-aware KV Cache optimization for multimodal large language models.

## Research Interests

- **Computer-using agents** - Web agents that reason about interactive environments and recover from incorrect or uncertain actions.
- **World models for action correction** - Predictive models that help agents evaluate possible consequences before and after acting.
- **Efficient multimodal inference** - Long-context multimodal inference methods, especially modality-aware KV Cache optimization for MLLMs.
