## About Me

Hi! I'm **Tianqi Liu (刘天琦)**, a first-year M.S. student at AI4GC Lab, Zhejiang University, advised by [Shengyu Zhang](https://person.zju.edu.cn/shengyuzhang). I received my B.S. in Software Engineering from Tongji University.

My research centers on **recommender systems** and **LLM agents**, with a focus on **model customization, lightweight deployment, and reinforcement-learning-based refinement**. More recently I've become interested in **personalized, user-centric LLM agents** and the challenge of proactive interaction in human–agent collaboration.

I'm always happy to chat about device-cloud collaborative intelligence and GUI/computer-use agents — feel free to reach out via email.

## Research Interests

- **Efficient & personalized recommendation** — customizing and compressing on-device models for individual users through device-cloud collaboration.
- **LLM / GUI agents** — interpretable, RL-refined multimodal agents for computer-use and human-in-the-loop tasks.
- **Personalized, user-centric agents** — proactive interaction and adaptation in human–agent collaboration.
